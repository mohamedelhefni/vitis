## About Vitis

**Vitis** (Latin for "grapevine") is a Hugo theme that helps your ideas grow and interconnect naturally. Just as grapevines create intricate networks of branches, Vitis helps you build a rich network of interconnected content.

### Features

- **Knowledge Graph Visualization** - See how your content connects through an interactive D3.js graph
- **Bidirectional Links** - Create wiki-style links between pages and automatically track backlinks
- **Series Support** - Organize related posts into educational series with progress tracking
- **Beautiful Green Theme** - Inspired by nature with a calming, vine-inspired color palette
- **Dark Mode** - Seamlessly switch between light and dark themes
- **Fast & Lightweight** - Built with Tailwind CSS for optimal performance
- **Typography First** - Clean, readable typography optimized for long-form content

### Perfect For

- 🌱 **Digital Gardens** - Cultivate your ideas and let them grow organically
- 📚 **Documentation Sites** - Create interconnected technical documentation
- ✍️ **Personal Blogs** - Share your thoughts with beautiful presentation
- 🎓 **Learning Notes** - Build your personal knowledge management system
